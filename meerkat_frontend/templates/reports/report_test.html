More Information

￼A program of primary care and public health surveillance is being implemented in {{ report.data.clinic_num }} outpatient clinics in {{ report.data.project_region|e }} (every MoH hospital, prison, mental health facility and 80% of primary and comprehensive centres), in partnership with WHO and Ministry of Health. The project introduces case-based, integrated disease surveillance of mental health, non-communicable disease and communicable disease.

The clinician uses the system within the consultation, which introduces clinical-decision support for child health and mental health, as well as best practice prescribing guidance and and real-time reporting of information. Information is made available within one hour via an online framework with automated generation of SMS and email alerts and support for mapping and reporting.
